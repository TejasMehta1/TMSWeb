Meet Our Team!










President


Hey look! That's me at the interface of the solution and the stirring rod!


Rishi Shridharan

aka Bob Roshi (Dr. Sishi)

What’s up, doc? Voltaire said “The art of medicine consists in amusing the patient while nature cures the disease.” Our current philosophy of medicine is a little bit different :-) But that’s what makes medicine so fascinating! Using imperfect sciences (anatomy, physiology, biology, etc.), we are finding solutions to ailments of a perfect construction (the human body). Outside of talking medicine (of course), ask me any time and I’d love to hang out. I love to talk [and ramble about my research], dance [badly, but as they say “dance like nobody’s watching”], play piano & trombone, cook, volunteer, and do a lot more. Also, if you liked the TV show MASH, we’re going to have a blast talking about it! Generally, if you’d like to do something together, I’m up for it! It’s my job to set the vision for TMS next year. You know that mission statement? That’s what we’re trying to do, and I’m going to do my best to make sure we can do it. But nothing can work as a one man show! My board is absolutely phenomenal and I am confident that you’ll find something you resonate with in every one of us. We’re here for different reasons and we have different aims, but TMS brought us together and each of us has a special love of medicine which we want to share with YOU! By the way, I will not hesitate to shamelessly advertise TMS events so get ready for a busy & exciting year with many amazing events planned! Vice President


Surya Donty

aka “Cure” ya (Dr. Durya)

Hey guys! I guess this is where I introduce myself, so here goes! It would honestly be easier to list the things I do not like, but that would be a pretty negative list. I like hanging out with with my friends, listening to music, playing tennis, watching TV series like Frasier and The Office, playing cello, and tons of other things that would just make this page a huuuggeee list of stuff. Something interesting about me is that I’m from this place called Longview (if you haven’t heard of it then that’s not shocking), which is really east in Texas. I’d like to think I’m a fairly approachable and laid-back person, but even if I turn out not to be in your opinion, don’t hesitate to talk and say hey! I love talking and meeting new people! Honestly my board is too amazing, and if you thought their bios were good then wait till you meet them in person. As Vice President of TMS, my main job is to handle all things committees; if you can think it and it relates to a committee then I most likely do it. TMS changed me a lot in a positive way, so I hope we can have that same effect on you guys through your TAMS year!
